Dream - Candelion

This album is a portrayal of love in its various dimensions, capturing a journey from initial attraction, to loss, to reunion, and finally, fulfillment. Through its songs, the album reveals both the pure, warm aspects of love and the bittersweet pain of separation and self-reflection. As the story unfolds, love grows from innocent admiration into a deeper commitment, ultimately becoming an essential, stabilizing force in each other's lives.
