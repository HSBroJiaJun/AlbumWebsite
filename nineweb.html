Brat - Charli XCX

Charli xcx’s redefinition of “brat” pivots from the traditional view of a petulant child. The new brat is a more complex and textured portrayal of rebellion and idiosyncrasy. In her view, a “brat” embodies a spirit of nonconformity and audacity. This new definition aligns with the ethos of youthful defiance and a unique, unabashed approach to creativity and self-expression.
