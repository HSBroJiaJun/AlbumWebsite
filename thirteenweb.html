Short n’ Sweet
Sabrina Carpenter

In a summer teeming with charting pop hits, Sabrina Carpenter reigns above all with the witty, raunchy and oh-so-cheeky Short n’ Sweet. Her sixth studio album chronicles the disaster that is the modern dating scene, artfully blending fun, dirty-minded humor with not-so-subtle jabs at bad boyfriends and fakes.
