Gemini Rights
Steve Lacy

Gemini Rights is essentially me coming into myself after a breakup. To me, the subject matter could be sad if you look at it that way, but I feel like it is way more hopeful. I’m finding myself after a heartbreak and I am excited for people to hear this record.Even with "Sunshine" a song with myself and Fousheé, I feel like that song is perfect.
